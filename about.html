About Me

I am a UI Designer
&

Web Developer

I’m Presley Tempies, a UI Designer and Front‑End Web Developer dedicated to creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I specialize in crafting responsive interfaces that feel intuitive and engaging, supported by clean, scalable code. I enjoy transforming concepts into interactive designs—focusing on usability, accessibility, and thoughtful visual design. Whether I’m shaping the early stages of a layout or refining the final pixel, I aim to produce work that makes a meaningful impact. I’m always exploring new design approaches, improving workflows, and expanding my skills to stay aligned with modern web standards. My goal is simple: create digital work that feels effortless for users and powerful for brands.

What I do:

UI & Visual Design

I design clean, modern, and user friendly interfaces that elevate user experience and strengthen brand identity.

Front End Web Development

I build responsive, high performance websites using HTML, CSS, and best practice front end techniques.

UX Research & User Centric Design

I ensure every website adapts beautifully to desktops, tablets, and mobile screens, maximizing usability and engagement.

Brand & Digital Identity Alignment

I help align visual styles, colors, and layouts with brand personality, ensuring a consistent and recognizable digital presence.
